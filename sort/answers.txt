sort1 uses: Bubble

How do you know?: Bubble is generally slower since in order to sort array bubble sorting method requires more swaps than selection.

sort2 uses: Merge

How do you know?: Merge sorting method is generally faster than other 2 methods, while using more memory. It achieves this by constantly dividing array so it can work with smaller arrays.

sort3 uses: Selection

How do you know?: Selection needs fewer swaps than bubble.
